Automation is the key to trading success on the stock exchange

Many people believe that trading on the cryptocurrency market is an extremely risky activity, cannot be considered as a stable source of income and generally requires some specific knowledge, skills, even a special psychological makeup. Well, maybe they are right in some ways, but this applies to all other spheres of human activity: who would argue that working as a taxi driver or security guard in a supermarket is absolutely safe, profitable and one hundred percent suitable for anyone? Nevertheless, we strive to objectively minimize risks and eliminate the influence of the notorious "human factor" on the workflow, and, as you know, automation is the key means of achieving this goal. It is the automation of everything and everything that has become a cardinal direction of the development of society, in which the value of human life is not disputed. The role of man in a fully automated environment is reduced to the realization of creativity, moral improvement, expansion and deepening of knowledge about the laws of the universe. Based on such ideas, we take an approach to the process of profiting from fluctuations in the exchange rate of market assets as a type of activity that can be easily automated, and strive to eliminate human interference from this activity as completely as possible, inevitably associated with making mistakes and miscalculations caused by emotions, lack of time, distraction, fatigue and other factors inherent in humans and completely absent from the mechanism. We implement a certain algorithm based on the most fundamental laws of the market (namely, its fundamental unpredictability and cyclicity), and embody it in the form of software suitable for effective profit-making, which, once configured and put into operation, no longer requires any control actions on the part of a human operator. We also try to make the user's interaction with the software as simple and intuitive as possible, which does not require deep special knowledge and does not allow critical errors in case of incorrect user actions. This is the purpose of our development. Thanks for attention. We wish you all creative success and material well-being!
